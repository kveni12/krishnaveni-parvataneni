About

I work at the intersection of machine learning, medicine, and responsible decision-making. My goal is to translate complex clinical workflows into practical technical systems, from LLM-powered extraction pipelines to predictive risk models.

I completed my B.S. in Artificial Intelligence and Decision-Making at MIT and am pursuing an M.Eng. in Computer Science (Bio-EECS concentration), with a focus on deployable healthcare AI systems.

I am especially interested in translational projects where thoughtful modeling, careful data engineering, and clinician collaboration can improve care quality and accessibility in high-stakes environments.
